From The Owner's Desk
A good teacher can make a world of difference in a student's life, impacting everything from their classroom learning to their long-term success. If you're considering a career in education, it's supreme to explore the qualities of a good teacher.
Research shows that a good teacher is a crucial factor that contributes to a student's achievement in the classroom, more important than facilities, school resources, and even school leadership.
